Why it matters

Wrong-bin waste is a bigger problem than it looks

Nearly a quarter of household recycling gets rejected at sorting facilities because of contamination and misclassification — most of it from simple, honest confusion about what goes where.

About GreenSort AI

A hackathon project with a real-world job to do

GreenSort AI was built to remove the single biggest barrier to good recycling: not knowing what a thing is actually made of. It pairs a lightweight image classifier with a plain-language disposal guide, so the right answer is always one photo away.
